# Online Retail Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview
A business sales analytics project analysing 541,909 e-commerce 
transactions from a UK-based online retailer (Dec 2010 – Dec 2011).
Built as part of the Future Interns Data Science & Analytics Task 1.

## Key Business Insights
- Total Revenue: £11M over 12 months
- Peak Month: November 2011 (£1.5M) — driven by Christmas demand
- Top Market: UK accounts for 85% of all revenue
- Top Product: Regency Cakestand 3-Tier (£174K revenue)
- Average Order Value: £534 per invoice

## Recommendations
1. Invest heavily in Q4 stock and marketing — revenue nearly 
   doubles in Oct-Nov
2. Expand internationally — Netherlands, Germany and EIRE show 
   strong growth potential
3. Investigate the 25% of orders with no CustomerID — a major 
   loyalty and retention gap
4. Run promotions in Feb-April to counter the seasonal revenue dip
